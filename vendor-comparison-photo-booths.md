## THEME MATCHING IDEAS

For "Black and White Classy" aesthetic:
- Black and white backdrop (curtain or floral wall)
- Elegant gold or silver props
- Monochrome photo strip template
- Script fonts for customization ("M + T" or "Just Married")
- No colorful silly props - keep it elegant
