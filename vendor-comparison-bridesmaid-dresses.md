## QUESTIONS TO ASK

1. Do you have sage green or olive green options?
2. What is your size range?
3. Can bridesmaids order different styles in the same color?
4. What is the shipping/production time?
5. Do you offer try-on samples?
6. What is your return/exchange policy?
7. Do dresses run true to size?
8. Are alterations typically needed?
