## WHAT IS A WEDDING CONTENT CREATOR?

Unlike traditional videographers who create cinematic films, content creators specialize in:
- **Vertical iPhone-style videos** for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Stories
- **Behind-the-scenes moments** captured candidly
- **Quick turnaround** - typically 24-72 hours after wedding
- **Social media-ready content** you can share immediately

**NOTE:** This is SEPARATE from and IN ADDITION TO your traditional videographer.
